*Texas Unclaimed Property Holder
**Webinars in 2025* 

The Comptroller’s office is pleased to offer free educational training webinars for holders [ https://comptroller.texas.gov/programs/unclaimed/?utm_source=govd&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=up-holder-pgrm&utm_content=taxp ] of unclaimed property. 

The webinars will focus on unclaimed property reporting requirements for general business entities, local government agencies (cities and counties), financial institutions (banks and credit unions), electric and telephone cooperatives, oil and gas companies, and businesses reporting securities.

We will discuss the following topics and more:


  * Unclaimed property *overview*. 
  * State *requirements* for reporting unclaimed property. 
  * Your *responsibilities* as a holder. 
  * Use of correct *report types* for your company. 

We also will offer free training on how to use our Manual Online Reporting [ https://www.claimittexas.gov/mor/login ] (MOR) system.
